How to Play:

Click "New Game" to start. The first hole will start and you will see a ball on a blue mat, like below.


Click anywhere on the mat to drop the ball where you want it to be placed. Once you have dropped the ball, your mouse becomes the putter and an arrow will appear in the opposite direction from where your mouse is. Use the arrow to determine the speed and angle at which the ball will be hit.

Once you have determined the direction and speed at which you want the ball to go, click again to hit the ball.

Continue this until you have sunk the ball in the hole! Good luck!
